[[caption]]

[[caption]] is a dynamically animated slide theme featuring falling snow accumulating at the bottom. The backgrounds are generated randomly, with colors typical of christmas, and randomly generated stars.

You can use this theme directly, or as a starting point for your own animated slide designs.

[[include "/themes/Slides/SlideTheme.theme.html"]]

Implementation

The theme [[caption]] is implemented in the Tao application directory under modules/seasons_greeings.
